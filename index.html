About

Whiplash is a drum band that implements jazz musicals in a modern setting.
Formed in 2003 they have emerged as one of the most influential music groups in the industry. With a talented group of musicians and instrumentalists they have managed to make jazz "cool" again. They have won the grammy once and have been nominated multiple times over the years. This has cemented their place in music history.









Two time grammy nominees for the best album and song of the year.








Best New Song and Best New Group Winners.
